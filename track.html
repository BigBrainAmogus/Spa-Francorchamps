Track Overview

Start-Finish Line

Every lap of this famed track starts and finishes right here, on the start finish line. Every legend that has raced at this track has at one point passed over this point. Senna, Schumacher, Vettel, Hamilton; everyone of those people have started or ended there laps right here.

Eau Rouge and Raidillon

These two corners make up probably one of the most iconic sections of any track in the world; you can show this image to anyone interested in motorsport and they'll like be able to tell you both the name of the track as well as the name of this infamous, technical section. This high speed section challenges both the driver's skill and determination. Some of the most legenday overtakes of the Formula One season have occured here such as Pierre Gasly's overtake of Sergio Perez in 2019.

Spa Francorchamps is a premier race circuit located in Belgium. Containing legendary corners such as Eau Rouge and Radillion, the track is bathed in heritage.
