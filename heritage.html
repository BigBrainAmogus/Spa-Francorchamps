Spa Francorchamps

Spa Francorchamps initially opened in 1921 and has been a premier race track ever since. Find out some of the most memorable moments below

1921

Track Opened

Originally designed in 1921, Spa Francorchamps eventually opened as a race track in August 1921. There was an opening race scheduled however it was cancelled after unfortunately, only one driver entered.

1939

The creation of Eau Rouge

it was this year that possible the most famous corner in all of motor racing was constructed. In July of 1939, Raidillon and Eau Rouge were added to the track when the Customs Hairpin was removed.

1950

First Formula 1 Grand Prix at Spa

The first Belgian Grand Prix as part of the Formula 1 Season was held at Spa. It was the fifth race of the season and was won by Juan Manuel Fangio. Fangio also won the year after, once again beating Nina Farina for the win.

1960

One of the Deadliest Grand Prix Weekends

Unfortunately the 1960 Belgian Grand Prix was one of the deadliest weekends up until that point. Stirling Moss and Mike Taylor were injured during practice and Chris Bristow and Alan Stacey were killed during the race

1979

Major Redesign

After the race had been boycotted by both drivers and teams alike in 1969, the track underwent major alterations that almost halved its total distance but increased safety drastically. The Belgian Grand Prix would return to Spa in 1983

1992

First Win of Michael Schumacher

The first win of undoubtedly one of the greatest drivers of all time occured at Spa during the 1992 Belgian Grand Prix. Michael Schumacher achieved his first of a record breaking 92 wins at this race after entering Formula 1 the previous season.

1993

24 Hours of Spa Francorchamps Cancelled

Endurance racing had occured at spa regularly since 1964 although only one has ever been stopped mid race. In 1993, the race was stopped after 15 hours due to the death of the Belgian King Baudouin.

2019

First Win for Ferrari at Spa

Despite competiting in Formula 1 since its inception, 2019 was the first time that the team won at Spa Francorchamps with Charles Leclerc picking up his first victory and becoming the third youngest race winner of all time.

2021

Rain cancelled the 2021 Belgian Grand Prix

With heavy rain belting the track, the 2021 Belgian Grand Prix was delayed multiple times in hopes that the weather would eventually clear up. When it was evident that it wouldn't, two laps were completed behind the safety car and half points were awarded; the first time in history.

Spa Francorchamps is a premier race circuit located in Belgium. Containing legendary corners such as Eau Rouge and Radillion, the track is bathed in heritage.
